# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- assuming good intent
- discussing disagreements on the merits
- keeping collaboration professional

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or publishing private information
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation for conduct that harms the project or its contributors.

Contact `jakob@jait.dev` for conduct concerns.
